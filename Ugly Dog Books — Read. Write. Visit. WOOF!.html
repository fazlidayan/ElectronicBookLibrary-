Ugly Dog Books is a charming, independent bookstore in downtown Attleboro. We offer a relaxed, comfortable atmosphere for you to browse our book selection. We carry both new and used books. We have a trade-in policy for gently used books. If there is a title that you don’t see we can order it for you. All new titles (in-store and special order) are 10% off retail price and shipping generally takes 24-48 hours for book orders. We also offer unique gifts and cards for any occasion as well as some beautiful local artwork.

Ugly Dog Books is a local gathering place for readers, writers, artists and folks who love books! Come by and visit to get your WOOF of the day.

Ugly Dog Book Club

We meet the third Tuesday of every month. From 7pm-8pm. Only requirement is to purchase the book at UDB. You get 15% off the title.
